About Us

Midland Panic is a global rock and folk music band known for their eclectic blend of genres and energetic live performances. Formed in the heart of the Midwest, the band has gained a loyal following around the world with their unique sound and heartfelt lyrics.

Comprised of talented musicians with diverse musical backgrounds, Midland Panic brings a fresh perspective to the music scene, drawing inspiration from a wide range of influences including classic rock, folk, blues, and indie music.
